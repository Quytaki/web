  • About Da Nang

    Known as the most livable city in Vietnam, Da Nang is always on the "priority list" when talking about tourist destinations in Vietnam. People come to Da Nang for many things: a place where the sea - mountains - beautiful rivers meet, friendly people, and the modern pace of life of a city that is growing every day. Because of these things, Da Nang tourism always attracts many tourists

  • Dragon Bridge

    Dragon Bridge, also known as the Golden Bridge, is a prominent landmark in Da Nang, Vietnam. With its unique architecture, Dragon Bridge takes the form of a giant dragon stretching across the Han River, creating a spectacular sight. At night, the bridge is illuminated by thousands of colorful LED lights, creating a dazzling light display. Completed in 2013, it has quickly become a popular tourist attraction.

  • Ghenh Bang

    Ghenh Bang is a popular tourist attraction located in Da Nang, Vietnam. It is a rocky coastal area with stunning natural beauty and unique geological formations. Ghenh Bang is known for its picturesque scenery, with rugged rocks, rock formations, and crashing waves. Visitors can explore the area by walking along the cliffside paths, admiring the dramatic coastal scenery. It is also a great place to enjoy the sunset and witness the waves crashing against the rocks, creating a mesmerizing spectacle.

  • Linh Ung Pagoda

    Located on the Son Tra peninsula, the pagoda is famous for its beautiful architecture and panoramic views of the surrounding landscape. The main attraction of Linh Ung Pagoda is the impressive 67-meter tall statue of Lady Buddha, also known as the Goddess of Compassion. This white statue is considered the tallest statue in Vietnam and stands majestically overlooking the city and sea. Visitors to Linh Ung Pagoda can admire the intricate architectural details of the pagoda with intricate carvings and beautiful decorations. The peaceful atmosphere of the temple complex provides a peaceful setting for meditation and spiritual contemplation.

  • My Khe Beach

    Voted by Forbes magazine as one of the 6 most attractive beaches on the planet, My Khe Beachis a tourist destination Da Nang is attractive and you definitely cannot ignore it. This place has beautiful scenery, a long coastline, and clear, gentle blue sea water. My Khe Beach has been planned by the city to develop with many types of entertainment services. So when coming here, tourists, in addition to walking on the beach and swimming, can also experience many other interesting activities such as paragliding, surfing, scuba diving, etc.

  • Xuan Thieu Beach

    Xuan Thieu Beach is one of the attractive tourist destinations in Da Nang. Located in Xuan Thieu eco-tourism area. Traveling to Da Nang here, visitors can rent camping tents overnight, play jet skis, enjoy fresh seafood,... Especially, coming here, don't miss the opportunity to catch the sunrise and see the scenery. The fishermen of the fishing village cast their nets in the morning.

Tien Sa Beach

Da Nang tourist destination Tien Sa Beach is located in the north of Son Tra peninsula. Separated from the noisy city, this place has a wild, quiet beauty. The peaceful beach between the majestic Son Tra mountain is very suitable for resting and exploring. Remember not to miss fun activities such as paragliding, jetski, roller coaster, etc. when coming here.
