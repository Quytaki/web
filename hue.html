  • About Hue

    Hue Vietnam, the former imperial capital, boasts a long-lasting treasure trove of world-renowned ancient wonders, distinctive cultural traits, and delightful traditional cuisine, where visitors can immerse themselves in Vietnam's illustrious history as if they are traveling back in time.

  • Move

    You can travel to Huế by plane with ticket prices starting from 500,000 VND. If you want a more exciting experience, you can try traveling by train. It is one of the great means of transportation that allows you to enjoy the Hai Van Pass if you're coming from the direction of Đà Nẵng.
    After landing at Phu Bai Airport, you will need to travel about 15km to reach the center of Huế. You can choose one of two transportation options: taking a taxi at a cost of around 250,000 VND per trip, or using a shuttle bus from the airport to the center, which costs approximately 40,000 VND - 50,000 VND per passenger per way. To explore all the attractions in Huế at your own pace, don't forget to consider renting a motorbike for about 100,000 VND per day.

  • Attire when visiting Huế:

    Attire when visiting Huế is important because it is a place with many temples and pagodas. It is advisable to choose modest and conservative clothing to show respect. Otherwise, you may be denied entry. Don't forget to buy a conical hat (nón lá) at Đông Ba Market.

  • Suggested tourist destinations for a day in Huế:

    - Imperial City (Kinh thành Huế): The Imperial City of Huế is an architectural masterpiece recognized as a UNESCO World Cultural Heritage site. Built over two centuries ago, it still maintains its solemn and ancient beauty, making it a must-visit destination for a day trip in Huế. Estimated visit duration: 1-3 hours. Ticket price reference: 200,000 VND per adult.

  • Thiên Mụ Pagoda

    Located about 5km from the city center, Thiên Mụ Pagoda sits on Hà Khê Hill, overlooking the picturesque Perfume River. The ancient and elegant architecture, including the stone turtle and the seven-story Phước Duyên Tower, will leave a lasting impression on visitors.

  • Thủy Tiên Lake's Mystical Beauty

    RSurrounded by lush green mountains, Thủy Tiên Lake exudes a mystical and enchanting ambiance. The giant white dragon "embracing the pearl" in the middle of the water is a popular spot for photography. Don't miss the opportunity to capture some impressive shots here.

Đông Ba Market and Tràng Tiền Bridge:

Đông Ba Market has long been a bustling trading hub and symbolizes the vibrant commercial atmosphere of Huế. Visitors come here to explore, shop, and indulge in Huế's local cuisine. The market offers diverse items, including fashion, handicrafts, souvenirs, and local specialties to bring home as gifts.

Must-Try Delicacies in Huế

Cơm hến/bún hến: A unique dish with simple ingredients such as rice or vermicelli noodles, stir-fried baby clams, spring onions, bean sprouts, roasted peanuts, and herbs. It's a delightful culinary experience with prices ranging from 10,000-20,000 VND per bowl. Additionally, there are numerous other options to satisfy your taste buds, such as bún bò Huế (Huế-style beef noodle soup), bánh canh (thick noodle soup), bún chả (grilled pork with vermicelli), bánh mì ốp la (baguette with fried egg), with prices varying from 7-50,000 VND per serving, depending on the dish and the selling location.
