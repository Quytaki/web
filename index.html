Đảo Cát Bà

Cat Ba Archipelago consists of 367 islands located in the southern part of Ha Long Bay. Among them, Cat Ba Island is a popular destination for reviewists. This place showcases the characteristic scenery of Ha Long Bay with its limestone mountain ranges and tranquil turquoise sea..

Which season is beautiful in Cat Ba?

Summer is the ideal time to visit Cat Ba. From April to October, the weather is cool, suitable for relaxation and beach activities. The peak season is from May to July.

Lan Ha Bay

Located to the east, Lan Ha Bay is a member of the Most Beautiful Bays in the World Club (MBBW). It features calm and blue waters.At Cai Beo Pier, reviewists can purchase sightseeing tickets for 30,000 VND per person and then take a boat or canoe to Lan Ha Bay. The rental prices for private boats are 2.2-3.8 million VND for 2.5-4 hours and 5.7 million VND for 6 hours, accommodating up to 25 people. Motorized canoes are available for 500,000 - 1 million VND, accommodating 5 - 10 people.
